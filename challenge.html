In 2016 the United Nations put forth 17 sustainable development goals to prevent drastic changes in the climat by the end of 2030.One of these goals is based on responsible consumption and prodduction. Already in 2007 the Norwegian government wrote a document where they had a goal for Norway to be the leading country for sustainable development. To reach this goal the population of Norway needs to recycle their waste so it can become new materials in an efficient and eco-friendly way. But still in 2020 we are not good enough to recyle.
PICTURE
In 2019 each citisen of Norway prodused 427 kg of waste. The same year only 173,9 kg of waste per person got deliverd to recyling. Waste that does not get recycled gets sendt to a facility where they burn it all to produce heat and it becomes useless ash. That is why it is important to recycle so this material does not og to waste, but rather can be recreated into other products. This save the enviroment from the exration of raw material.
