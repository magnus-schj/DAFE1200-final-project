The solution

[app name] contributes to responsible consumption by...

  • Making your responsible consumption visible

  • Giving you an instant pay-off for your efforts

The concept of the app is that you register what you recycle, and get rewarded points. For example, if you have a broken TV that you recycle through our app, you earn an amount of points that you can use for various goods and deals provided by our partners. This can be anything from a free movie ticket, to a chance of winning a car.


The App will also contain several features. You can engage in daily, weekly, or monthly challenges to earn extra points. You can add your frineds, and check out how much they have recycled through a "friends"-leaderboard (There will also be a global leaderboard). This makes recycling go from being a chore, to a fun contest where you actually can win something, while at the same time saving the earth!

Here is an example of how it will work:

You have waste that need recycling

You bring it to one of our recycling hubs or a supported store, and register the recycled items

You get points, and can use theese on whatever you want in our shop!

Our responsibillity

It is important for us to keep the process sustainable, even after you are done recycling. We have strict requirements to our partners of how they handle their part. For example, we will not start a partnership with an organization that will handle the waste in a un-sustainable, dangerous, or otherwise irresponsibly way. Of our partners that provide rewards and perks, we requiere that they have a good history with being responsible and sustainable. We will never offer rewards that may lead to unresponsible consumtion, or that is unproper in any other way.
