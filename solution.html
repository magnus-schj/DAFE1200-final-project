The solution

[app name] contributes to responsible consumption by...

  • Making your responsible consumption visible
  • Giving you an instant pay-off for your efforts

Make a difference with recycling through [app name]. [App name] is an app that gives you points when you register that you have recycled a product correctly. If you for example have a broken TV screen, and recycle it responsibly, you get a certain amount of point that you can use to get various perks, provided by our partners. This can be anything from a free movie ticket to the chance of winning a new car.

There are also several features that keep people engaged, like daily, weekly, and monthly challenges with rewards for the top contestants. This feature contributes to making a chore a fun and engaging contest. In addition, you can see where you are on a local, global, and a friend based leaderboard. Here, you make your contribution visible to others, and you can see the effort of others. You will be able to see how much of an impact you and your friends have made as a whole.
