Problem

Ensure sustainable use of resources with recycling!

In 2016 the United Nations put forth 17 sustainable development goals to prevent drastic changes in the climat by the end of 2030. One of these goals is based on responsible consumption and prodduction. Already in 2007 the Norwegian government wrote a document where they had a goal for Norway to be the leading country for sustainable development. To reach this goal the population of Norway needs to recycle their waste so it can become new materials in an efficient and eco-friendly way. In 2020, we are still not recycling good enough.

In 2019 each citizen of Norway produced 427 kg of waste, while just 173,9 kg of waste per person was deliverd to recyling. Waste that does not get recycled gets sendt to a facility where they burn it all to produce heat and it becomes useless ash. It is important to recycle so that this material does not go to waste, but rather can be recreated into other products. This saves the environment from the extraction of raw material.

Why do people not recycle?

Green Point Norway did a survey on Norwegian citizens on why they do not recycle. A frequent answer was that we do not get anything back for the time spent on recycling. Check out our solution to this problem.
