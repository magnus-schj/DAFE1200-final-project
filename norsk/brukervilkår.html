Privacy and terms

We at “PawnPoint” value your privacy, and as a company we want to give you the best user experience. Therefore, we collect data from our users without breaching your privacy. By using our services, you agree to our terms of service. If you want to know what we do with the data we collect, read how we responded to our most frequent questions below.

Do you need to access my position?

To give you (the user) the best user experience you have the option to select GPS tracking. This is necessary if you want to see your location in relation to our services. You have the option to turn this off in your settings page on your device. The position data we collect is encrypted and will not be accessed by any of our partners nor our employees. The location-based data will be deleted every 30 days to make sure that this information can’t be accessed by anyone.

What information do we share with our partners?

We share necessary account information with our partners. This information contains your name and your email. With this information, our partners have the ability to give you discounts on different products. GPS tracking and other location-based information will not be given to our partners as this is not considered as necessary.

How do I remove information about myself?

All data we collect is deleted every 30 days. If you want to remove personal information about yourself, you can go to your settings page in the app where you have the option to delete your account. If you have any questions about removing your information or deleting your account, you can contact our support team here: support@PawnPoint.no.
