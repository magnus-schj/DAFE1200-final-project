[FORKLARER HVORDAN APPEN FUNKER]

Make a difference with recycling through [App name]. [App name] is an app that gives you points when you register that you have recycled a product correctly. If you for example have a broken TV screen, and recycle it responsibly, you get a certain amoubnt of point that you can use to get various perks, provided by our partners. This can be anything from a free movie ticket, to the chance of winning a new car.

There are also several features that keep peopple engaged, for example daily, weekly, and monthly challenges with rewards for the top contestants This feature contributes to making a chore to a fun and engaging contest. In addition, you can see where you are on a local, global, and a friend-leaderboard. Here, you make your contribution visible to others, and you can see the effort of others. You will be able to see how mucjh of an impact you and all your friends have made as a whole.
