Our Solution

How [app name] is a part of the solution

Our Goal

A part of the United Nations’ goal of responsible consumption and production focuses on resource efficiency. This implies that the resources used to make different products must be recycled so the material is not lost and can be reused. Norway already has a solution to recycle plastic bottles, but there are still problems with other kinds of waste. That is why our goal is to make it more attractive for the general public to recycle. Our goal is to make Norway better at recycling and becoming more resource efficient. As soon as we have made a difference in the Norwegian population, we will branch out across the world.

The solution

As mentioned on our problem site, a lot of people don’t recycle because they get nothing in return. That is why our company has developed a method to repay the people who recycle in different ways. We want to give back to the people who are doing a great service to our society and our planet.


Our app will give the people an option to get discounts on different products and even win prizes by recycling their waste. By using our app, you can locate our nearest recycling station. Here you can scan your waste and put them in the right containers. Depending on the type of waste you recycle, you will be given points which you can use on discounts given to us by our sponsors. You can also use your points to win small or big prizes from our lottery. If you want to know more about how the app works, check out our app page.

The waste that we collect will be transported to stations that will handle the waste in an efficient and environmentally friendly way. All the material will then be sold back to companies so that they can make new products. By doing this we will create a circle of renewable resources that can be used over and over. This makes it unnecessary for production of new resources and extraction of raw material.

Our responsibillity

It is important for us to keep the process sustainable, even after you are done recycling. We have strict requirements to our partners of how they handle their part. For example, we will not start a partnership with an organization that will handle the waste in a un-sustainable, dangerous, or otherwise irresponsibly way. Of our partners that provide rewards and perks, we requiere that they have a good history with being responsible and sustainable. We will never offer rewards that may lead to unresponsible consumtion, or that is unproper in any other way.
