Making recycling more rewarding and fun!

As of now, recycling is not very rewarding. It is very time consuming, and you don’t really feel that it makes any difference, or that anyone sees it. With the app, both you and everyone else can see how much of a difference you have made. You also get rewarded for recycling, with points that you can use exchange in the in app store page.
